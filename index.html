Frequently Asked Questions

What are your prices?

The average model costs $300 - $500+. This is an estimate, fill out the commission form for an exact estimate.

!!Commission Form Link!!

Am I locked in?

You may choose to forfiet your postion at any time if you decide not to continue with the commission process. Although, the first payment is non-refundable (see the commission form for addional information).

How does payments work?

All transactions will be conducted through the Paypal invoice system. A first payment of 50% is due before I start working on your model. The last payment 50% is due upon completion, files will be sent out after both payments have been made.

Made from Scratch?

All of the custom 3D models I make are made from scratch and are unique to each avatar. This means that I will NEVER use bases or vriod to make my models.

When will my VRChat Model be complete?

On average the model will take 2 month to complete, after I accept your commission.

What is a good example of concept art?

Good concept art will have the full body front, back, and side view. Any additional accessories should also be detailed. Feel free to included additional references.
